About PVAM Band

The Mount Vernon Performing and Visual Arts Band has had a tradition of excellence since the opening of our school in 2014. The band program currently consists of the show band, jazz band, percussion ensemble, rock band, and pep band. The PVAM Band is a source of pride in our community. We are commited to excellence in music, showmanship, and personal conduct. We build our program on seven core values: selflessness, integrity, respect, peace, loyalty, unity, and maturity. We believe this value system helps shape our students in to strong leaders for tomorrow.
